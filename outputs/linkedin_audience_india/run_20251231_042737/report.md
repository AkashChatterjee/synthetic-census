The insight hit hard: Users who wanted to leave were ALREADY gone mentally. The friction just made them hate us. But the easier flow gave us actual feedback, and 40% of "cancellers" reached out when we fixed their issues.

Best part? Our NPS from churned users went from 12 to 67. These same people now refer others and some have returned.

Dark patterns feel like growth hacks. But trust is the only moat that matters in B2B SaaS.

Sometimes the best retention strategy is making it stupid simple to leave.
